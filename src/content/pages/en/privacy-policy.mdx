This privacy notice describes the methods of processing personal data of users who visit the Hero Hubs website. The notice is provided pursuant to Article 13 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

### Types of data collected

The Hero Hubs website is a showcase site and does not automatically collect identifiable personal data. However, we may collect the following data in some circumstances:

- Navigation Data: During site navigation, some technical data are automatically collected, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and visited pages. These data are collected solely for anonymous statistical analysis and to improve the site.
- Voluntarily Provided Data: If you choose to contact us via contact forms or email, we may collect your name, email address, and other personal information you decide to provide to respond to your request.

### Purpose of processing

Personal data provided by users are processed exclusively for the following purposes:

- Responding to information requests
- Improving the browsing experience on the website
- Ensuring the correct functioning of the site and preventing possible abuses

### Processing methods

Personal data are processed with automated tools for the time strictly necessary to achieve the purposes for which they were collected. We adopt adequate security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

### Cookies

The site uses technical cookies to ensure the correct functioning of the pages and improve user experience. We do not use profiling cookies without the explicit consent of users. For more details on the cookies used, please refer to our cookie policy.

### Data sharing

The collected data will not be transferred to third parties nor used for commercial purposes without your consent. They may be shared with third-party service providers (such as hosting or analysis services) only if strictly necessary for the site's operation and always in compliance with current regulations.

### User rights

Pursuant to the GDPR, users have the right to:
Access their personal data;
Request the rectification or deletion of data;
Limit or object to the processing of their data;
Request data portability.
To exercise these rights, you can contact us at the email address provided in the contacts section.

### Changes to the privacy policy

This notice may be updated periodically. Changes will be published on this page, and it is recommended to consult it regularly to stay informed about any changes.

### Contacts

For any questions regarding this notice or the processing of your personal data, you can contact us at the address: info@herohubs.it
